About Me

After spending years in various industries including customer relationship management, account management, marketing, sales, customer service, and professional motocross racing, I recently retrained as a certified data analyst. I have always loved working with computers and technology, and I am always wanting to learn more and more. As a dedicated professional with a proven track record in diverse industries, I am now transitioning into the dynamic field of data analytics. I am eager to apply a robust foundation in problem-solving, critical thinking, and attention to detail to contribute effectively to the data science initiatives and analytics projects. I am seeking a challenging role that fosters continuous learning, allowing for the acquisition of new skills and certifications to excel in this rapidly evolving technological landscape.

My key skills include:

  • Collecting verbal information and interpreting business requirements documents (BRD).
  • Descriptive, Inferential, Predictive, and Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA).
  • Statistical analysis and hypothesis testing.
  • Data mining, cleaning, and wrangling.
  • Advanced Excel for statistical analytics, such as: PivotTables, VLOOKUP, Joins, VBA, MACROS, Forecasting.
  • Data visualization and dashboards using Tableau.
  • Database management, Relational Databases, Extract Transform Load (ETL), and queries using SQL.
  • Python data science programming, using ANACONDA Jupyter notebook and libraries such as Pandas, NumPy, SciPy, Seaborn, Matplotlib, Plotly, Folium, Geoplot, Apache Spark, Hadoop, Scikit-learn, and Quandl.
  • Supervised machine learning: Regression (Decision Trees and Random Forests).
  • Unsupervised machine learning: Clustering (Centroid, Density, Distribution, and Hierarchical).
